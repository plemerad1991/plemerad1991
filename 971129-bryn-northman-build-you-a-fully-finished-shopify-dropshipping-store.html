Build You A Fully Finished Shopify Dropshipping Store











About:

Hello



I will produce a fully functioning Shopify dropshipping store for a product/products of your choice, in your chosen niche.



The best outcome comes from great communication. Tell me exactly what you want, even if you think it is a minor detail let me know. I will keep in contact with you throughout the production.



A few things to you need to know when you contact me. 

  1. The niche, and the product/products you want to sell. I can choose for you, however, conversions are easier to come by if you know about your product.
  2. The name you would like to use for your store, subject to availability on Shopify. (I will get as close to the name a possible if the store already exists)
  3. The email address you want the store to be linked with.


The 1 product shop will come with

  1. 1 x application (obrerlo)
  2. 1 x Home page
  3. 1 x Checkout page
  4. Logo creation
  5. Full product description.


The 3/5 product Shop will come with

  1. 5 x applications (obrerlo, GDPR banner, Trust Badges, Ultimate Sales Boost, Currency Converter)
  2. 1 x Home page
  3. 1 x Checkout page
  4. 1 x About Us page
  5. 1 x Privacy policy page
  6. 1 x Terms of service page 
  7. 1 x Contact Us page
